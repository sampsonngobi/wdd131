Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this project is to provide a page where music lovers, fans and the general public will be able to access information about the artist SNC, get information about events and and upcoming releases, buy machandize and also access links to stream platforms to listen to my original songs and covers.

Audience

This website is build to serve anyone who enjoys listening to music of different genres. It is also for thos who will be discovering me as an artist, who will like to support me through the purchase of machandize, on line streeming and download of my songs. It is also for anyone who like to book me for shows locally or internationally.
